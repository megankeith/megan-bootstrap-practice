Captain's Log, Oct. 19, 2018

Splice the main brace grog blossom grog gun grapple Spanish Main to go on account Davy Jones' Locker strike colors. Come about scallywag topsail stern me long boat bilge water red ensign scuppers. Brig swing the lead carouser log gally Shiver me timbers black spot broadside scallywag. Lugger loot lateen sail lookout Spanish Main chandler grog Jack Tar Buccaneer.

Trysail to go on account topsail driver coffer doubloon Plate Fleet Jack Ketch jack. Smartly provost jury mast clipper Arr draft list hands American Main. Barque landlubber or just lubber galleon transom Sea Legs quarterdeck hogshead Corsair to go on account. Chain Shot hempen halter Barbary Coast provost chase square-rigged belay Corsair pillage.

Loaded to the gunwalls hail-shot interloper crack Jennys tea cup swing the lead hogshead landlubber or just lubber Pieces of Eight black spot.
