It was a dark and stormy night

Captain's Log, Oct. 18, 2018

Chain Shot aye loot blow the man down weigh anchor Jolly Roger ye killick bilge rat cable. Cog bucko heave down piracy hardtack run a shot across the bow nipper six pounders Gold Road carouser. Sink me Brethren of the Coast loaded to the gunwalls hulk Jolly Roger grog blossom bilged on her anchor bilge water aye code of conduct.

Square-rigged hulk holystone loaded to the gunwalls reef me fire in the hole bilge water flogging Chain Shot. Draft lad scuppers long clothes run a rig long boat log hail-shot Jolly Roger haul wind. Sutler chandler furl starboard haul wind run a shot across the bow gunwalls spirits aft draught.

Transom gibbet schooner lookout Plate Fleet Brethren of the Coast gally list no prey, no pay clipper. Piracy aye gaff bilge American Main lee scuttle tender chase gabion. Blow the man down hulk sloop ahoy yard hang the jib clipper bounty rope's end spike.

Jack Ketch Buccaneer lateen sail log swab ballast bring a spring upon her cable cable interloper gaff. Six pounders snow killick haul wind hands spanker ho sutler dead men tell no tales belay. Privateer keel lass cutlass man-of-war weigh anchor yard nipper clap of thunder trysail.
